{% trans 'The aim of creating this website' %}

This website is made to share the whole information about Uzbek nationality and traditions. Uznation.inha.uz-national site is always ready to give help for users, volunteers, local people so that the people who really want to get a big source on Uzbekistan and its nationality can be satisfied with our work. In this national website, there could be found a great amount of useful information, collections of amazing facts and much more on historical places, sights where tourists can visit, monuments, architectural as well as decorative arts and crafts, famous writers, scientists, poets, heroes who created the history of our land and then praised them all over the world, regions and their administrative departs, national customs, habits, folklore of literature of Uzbekistan. Besides that, our staff never denies receiving your valuable comments and feedbacks is active which means a person who wishes to connect with us or say any suggestions for us can easily send us personal viewpoints. Since, the main goal of this work is to attract more users to spend these all among the world and making them aware about the history and values of independence Uzbekistan. As far the president of our country-I.Karimov said, “Uzbekistan should be one of the high countries that always get wonderful future”.
